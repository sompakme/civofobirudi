Mike Tyson’s Comfortable Abode

Mike Tyson's Seven Hills House is located in Henderson, Nevada which is just a few minutes from the Sin City. It was reportedly the only estate that the baddest boxer on the planet owns. This was after he reportedly tore his $300 million worth of wealth over the years on numerous homes, luxury cars and jewelries. Although this is the only estate he currently owns, it is considerably large estate on the average American standards. However for Hollywood celebrities whose usual estate averages around $20 million, it is considered low-end. The boxing champ bought the mansion which was listed by Luxury Homes of Las Vegas for $2.5 million.

The mansion is located on an almost acre of land in the upscale side of Seven Hills neighborhood which spans about 10,400 sq. ft. The estate includes two casitas which has one full apartment. The main house sits on 8,149 square-foot area which was built in 2001. It has spacious indoor and outdoor space which is something the guests will surely enjoy. The mansion is located behind a gated community of the Seven Hills where there is also a private golf club and a few leisure parks which serve the wealthy and affluent residents of the area. The previous house of the boxer is just right around the corner; he just bought this one because it is bigger and fits his requirements.

The estate has six luxurious and spacious bedrooms with six bathrooms which was styled in a Mediterranean style . The amenities on the property include a massive media room with wine cellar and two master suites. One of the master suites is located on the first floor while the other one is on the second floor. The interiors of the mansion has some Italian-style vibe which features some custom iron railings, beautiful coffered ceilings, stunning arched doorways, luxurious marble flooring, stone fireplaces, marble staircase and floor-to-ceiling windows.

The mansion has plenty of space in the closet which can fit some luxurious designer clothes. The mansion’s living room is well lit that has plenty of space. The kitchen in the mansion has some modern amenities as well and a fully loaded bar can be found on the mansion to entertain the guests coming over. Eating on the dining room of the mansion can serve as a pleasurable experience. There is also an outdoor barbeque area which makes the mansion an ideal place for entertaining guest with some al-fresco dining privately.
