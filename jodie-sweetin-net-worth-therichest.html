Jodie Sweetin is an American actress and television personality with a net worth of $800 thousand. She was born on January 19, 1982, in Los Angeles, California.

She started her career in 1987 by presenting a Jim Henson Presents Mother Goose Stories, which was a TV series. In the same year, she appeared in other several TV series known as Full House and The Hogan Family.

Since then, she has appeared in several television shows such as Party of Five, Yes, Dear, Farce of the Penguins, Redefining Love, Small Bits of Happiness, Can't Get Arrested, and Pants-Off Dance-Off. These shows contributed to her success in this entertainment industry.

She also won the Young Artist Award for the Outstanding Young Comedienne in the Television Series. In addition to that, she has also been nominated in the same category for 5 times. She has been married Shaun Holguin between the years 2002 to 2006, followed by Cody Herpin between 2007–2010 and Morty Coyle from 2012 to 2013.
