Benny Hinn Biography

Pastor Benny Hinn (born. Toufik Benedictus “Benny” Hinn December 3, 1952) is an Israeli-Canadian televangelist, teacher, and author who was born in Jaffa, Israel, and brought up in Ontario, Canada. The controversial faith healing evangelist is well known for his TV program This Is Your Day which is broadcast in 200 countries. Furthermore, he has gained huge popularity for his healing miracles in his church Miracle Crusade.

Moreover, he has authored numerous best-selling books such as Good Morning, Holy Spirit, Prayer That Gets Results, Blood in the Sand, and Lamb of God.

Benny Hinn Family

Bennywas born to both parents in a large-sized family in Jaffa, Israel. His father, Costandi Hinn served as mayor for Jaffa in 1952 which made him live his life as a loner away from other children. Nevertheless, his parents have Greek-Egyptian, Palestinian and Armenian-Lebanese ancestry though he is an Israeli national.

His mother Clemence Hinn was born in Palestine and died on May 3 2016, days before Mother’s Day. Benny has six siblings William Hinn, Sam Hinn, Henry Hinn, Michael Hinn, Chris Hinn, and Rose Hinn.

However, the Hinn family moved to Toronto, Ontario, Canada after the 1967 Arab–Israeli War.

Benny Hinn Brothers

Benny’s brother William Hinn is also a minister and founder of Resurrection Life. Sam Hinn has been followed with a couple of controversies like his brother Benny. He was accused of extramarital affairs back in 2013 which made him step down from the pulpit. However, he was reordained after admitting to the claims.

Moreover, his brother Henry Hinn is also a minister and founder of the Henry Hinn Ministries. However, Michael Hinn and Chris Hinn have maintained a low profile and very little is known about the two brothers.

Benny Hinn Wife

Pastor Benny is happily married to his lovely wifeSuzanne Harthern Hinn. Benny first married Suzanne Harthern on 4 Aug 1979 in a private wedding ceremony attended by close friends and family. The lovely couple is blessed with four children, three girls, and a son from their long-lasting marriage.

However, Suzanne Harthern filed for divorce on 1 Feb 2010. There were claims that Benny had an affair with televangelist Paula White which they both dismissed in the National Enquirer in July 2010. Moreover, Strang Communications sued Benny for violating the morality clause of his contract after his affair with Paula White.

Hinn claimed that the couple got divorced as a result of his wife’s addiction to prescribed drugs and his demanding schedule which did not leave enough time to spend with his family. Nevertheless, after several meetings and interventions, the former couple decided to get back together.

Benny announced that he and his ex-wife would remarry in Oct 2012. Pastor Hinn hitched Suzanne at the Holy Land Experience on 3 March 2013 in a traditional ceremony attended by a multitude of the congregation and Christian leaders.

Pastor Benny Hinn’s wife Suzanne Harthern Hinn is an American minister and speaker based in Florida.

His daughter Jessica Hinn was born on Dec 3, 1952, and has even previously gone to her father’s ministry and confessed that she is not born again. Natasha Hinn is a musician while Benny is grooming his son Josh Hinn to serve the Lord for eternity. Furthermore, he has a son-in-law called Michael Koulianos.

Pastor Benny Hinn Ministries

  • Benny is often referred to by his self-proclaimed name faith healer. While in Canada, he was a member of a singing troupe of young evangelicals as a teenager. Furthermore, he previously stated that evangelist Kathryn Kuhlman played a huge influence on how his life turned out. However, Dr. Winston I. Nunes taught pastor Benny the Bible and mentored him to be a pastor.
  • After learning the Bible, Benny moved to Orlando, FL where he started the Orlando Christian Center in 1983. It is in Orlando Christian Center that Benny began performing healing services claiming that God was using him as a healing vessel. His popularity increased greatly after he started holding Miracle Crusades in different stadiums in the US and the world.
  • Moreover, he started televising his miracle crusades in 1989. He also started the daily talk show This Is Your Day in 1990 which airs clips of Hinn’s Miracle Crusades. This Is Your Day was first aired on Trinity Broadcasting Network and ever since his ministry has grown to attract huge praises with equal criticism.
  • However, he stepped down as pastor of the Orlando Christian Center in 1999. He moved the Orlando Christian Center administrative headquarters to Grapevine, TX but hosts his daily 30-minute talk show This Is Your Day from a television studio in Orange County, CA. The World Healing Center Church mainly puts a focus on financial prosperity and is also behind the mission outreach programs.
  • Furthermore, This Is Your Day is one of the world’s most-watched Christian shows. It airs on multiple TV Networks besides the Trinity Broadcasting Network. This Is Your Day is televised on Daystar Television Network, INSP Networks, Vision TV,  The God Channel, Grace TV, Revelation TV and manages to reach 200 countries. Moreover, you can follow pastor Benny closely on the Benny Ministries website.
