Alfonso Borbolla also known as Poncho Borbolla is a Mexican actor, improvisor, and comedian. He is recognized for several projects like Disney’s “El Rey León México” and the comedy show “La Impro Lucha.”

Alfonso Borbolla came into the limelight with his recent cast in the TV series Bunker. Bunker is the first series produced by HBO Max in Mexico, an action-comedy series.

The actor is also famous for “Soy tu fan” and “Sr. Avila.” With a vast theatrical activity, Alfonso has also contributed to cinema: “El Fantastico Mundo De Juan Orol,” and recently, he was also cast in a series “Club de Cuervos” and “El Cesar.”
