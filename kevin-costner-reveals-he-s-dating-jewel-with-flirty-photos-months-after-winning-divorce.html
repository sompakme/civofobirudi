  • Kevin Costner appears to confirm romance with Jewel in flirtatious photos. They were seen together with Kevin having his arms around her waist.

  • Kevin and Jewel have traveled together to the British Virgin Islands for a tennis fundraiser. Jewel thanked Kevin on social media for being a mentor to her foundation.

Kevin Costner appears to have a new woman in his life, as the actor seemed to confirm his romance with singer Jewel after flirtatious photos of the pair were published.

Earlier this week, TMZ released photos showing the pair spending time together. The images show Jewel, 49, speaking into a microphone with her eyes on Costner, 68, who has his arms wrapped around her waist. The Yellowstone star had a huge smile on his face as he appeared to flirt with the blonde singer.

Kevin And Jewel Traveled To The Caribbean Last Month

While the images seem to imply a romantic connection between the pair, Kevin and Jewel have declined to comment on their suspected romance. But a source didn’t deny a relationship between the pair, telling TMZ it’s “rather new.”

“There was definitely something going on,” the source continued. “They were flirty. And when they were together, it was like they both just lit up.”

A different insider added, “You could tell they were trying to be discreet, but anybody who saw them could tell something was going on.”

It may be new, but Kevin and Jewel already appear to have traveled together. Page Six reports the duo were in the British Virgin Islands in November for a week to participate in a tennis fundraiser held by Jewel’s Inspiring Children Foundation.

Jewel went on to thank Kevin on social media for acting as a mentor to her foundation, though her post didn’t allude to any romantic connection between the two of them.

“Every year, my foundation @inspiringchildren and I go to Necker Island to help host a tennis event w @richardbranson to raise funds for our kids,” the Grammy winner wrote on Instagram. “Amazing players like @geniebouchard come and support us in playing doubles with the folks who come… it’s an incredible time, and one I use to relax, rest and play w my son!”

Kevin appears to be getting back into the dating game following his divorce from Christine Baumgartner. He was linked to Reese Witherspoon (who also went through a divorce from talent manager Jim Toth), though reps for the actors promptly denied the speculation.

Kevin’s Divorce Drama Is Finally Behind Him

Christine and Kevin were married for nearly two decades before she initiated divorce proceedings. A court ended up awarding Christine significantly less child and spousal support than she was originally seeking, despite the estranged couple signing a prenup prior to tying the knot.

Christine was initially awarded a temporary child support amount of $129,000 per month. Kevin also financially helped her move out of their shared family home (which the prenup stipulated would go to him in case of divorce, since he bought it a decade before their wedding).

Eventually, a judge ruled that Kevin is to pay a little over $63,000 a month for their three kids, significantly less than Christine was hoping to cash in at $248,000 per month.

Now that Kevin’s divorce drama is over, it appears the actor is moving on – and Jewel seems to be the new woman on his radar.
