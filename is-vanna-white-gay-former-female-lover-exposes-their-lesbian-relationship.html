Is Vanna White Gay?

No, Vanna White is not gay. Despite the rumors that have circulated about Vanna White’s sexuality, there is no concrete evidence to suggest that she is gay.

One of the sources of these rumors is an alleged affair between Vanna White and Liza Greer. Greer recounted an incident where White invited her over while her then-fiancé was away, and they had an intimate encounter.

In a 2019 interview with RadarOnline, Liza Greer said, “We were lesbian lovers and did what two people do in a bedroom. I had sex with her, obviously. It was pretty wild.”

However, it’s crucial to note that this isolated incident does not define someone’s sexual orientation. Vanna White has only publicly dated men throughout her life, and there have been no other substantiated reports of same-sex relationships.

At 66 years old, her dating history predominantly consists of relationships with men, which provides a clear indication of her heterosexual orientation.

Is Vanna White Married?

No, Vanna White is not married to anyone as of now but she has been dating contractor John Donaldson since 2012. The Wheel of Fortune hostess reportedly met her beau through mutual friends.

Before finding the love of her life, Vanna White had a series of relationships. In the 1980s, she dated and became engaged to John Gibson, who was not only a Playgirl magazine centerfold but also a Chippendales dancer who later pursued an acting career.

Tragically, Gibson lost his life in a plane crash in 1986. In 1990, Vanna White tied the knot with George Santo Pietro, a restaurant owner.

Their journey into parenthood began in September 1992 when White became pregnant. An episode of Wheel of Fortune even featured the answer “Vanna’s pregnant” as a puzzle.

However, she sadly suffered a miscarriage shortly after the episode was taped. White and Santo Pietro welcomed a son named Nicholas (often called Nikko) in 1994 and a daughter named Giovanna (nicknamed Gigi) in 1997.

Despite the initial joys of their family, the couple eventually divorced in November 2002.

Following her divorce, Vanna White was engaged to Southern California businessman Michael Kaye from 2004 to 2006, although they never made it to the altar.
