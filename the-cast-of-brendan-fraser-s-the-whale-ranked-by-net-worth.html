The Cast Of Brendan Fraser's The Whale, Ranked By Net Worth

The Whale is a psychological drama that tells the story of a man’s attempt to reconnect with his daughter, several years after abandoning his family to follow his lover. The movie, which is set to debut in December, made its world premiere at the 79th Venice International Film Festival on September 4 to a rousing 6-minute standing ovation. The movie promises to be intriguing, as early reviews pegged an approval rating of 85% for the psychological drama.

The critically acclaimed movie features eight main cast members, including Sadie Sink, Samantha Morton, and Brendan Fraser. The diverse cast members in the movie range from seasonal to upcoming actors with varying years of experience, and of course different pocket sizes. These cast members have earned a fortune from the devotion they put into their craft. Here’s how much the cast members on The Whale are worth.

8 Huck Milner Has A Net Worth Of $1 Million

Huck Milner plays the role of Ben on The Whale. The child star made his professional debut in 2018, years after he fell in love with community theater. After his role as the voice of Dash in Incredibles 2, the 15-year-old continued offering professional voice-over services and pursuing his passion for theater. Due to his age, the child star’s net worth has not been made public, but numerous sources project that Milner is worth approximately $1 million.

7 Sadie Sink Has A Net Worth Of $1 Million

Sadie Sink is worth $1 million. The child star kicked off her acting career at the age of seven with roles in local theater productions. In 2013, Sink progressed to TV with her role in The Americans, but it wasn’t until 2017 that the child star got her breakthrough role as Max Mayfield in Stranger Things. Since coming into the spotlight, Sink has been featured in notable projects, Fear Street, and All Too Well: The Short Film.

6 Ryan Heinke Has A Net Worth Of $1 Million

Ryan Heinke who plays the role of Dave on The Whale is worth $1 Million. The actor started his acting career in 2004 as a stunt performer in The Adventures of Sharkboy and Lavagirl 3-D. Over the length of his 18-years-long career, the child star featured in many supporting roles in numerous small screen projects on the small screen. The actor is known for his roles in The Santa Clause 3: The Escape Clause, Detention, and Glee.

5 Sathya Sridharan Has A Net Worth Of $1 Million

Sathya Sridharan, who portrays the role of Dan on The Whale has a net worth of $1 million. Sridharan embodies the world of arts. In the last decade, Sridharan has worked as a writer and an actor on screen and stage. The New York actor is best known for his roles in Minor Premise, Bikini Moon, and Little America. According to IMDB, Sridharan has 3 movie credits to his name with four projects currently in the post-production stage.

4 Ty Simpkins Has A Net Worth Of $2 Million

Ty Simpkins is worth $2 million. The actor, who made his TV debut when he was barely a month old has had a smooth transition from being a child star to taking on adult roles. Before starring in The Whales, Simpkins appeared in popular projects such as Insidious, Insidious: Chapter 2, and Jurassic World. According to IMDB, Simpkins has 37 movie credits to his name, with two movies in the pipeline.

3 Hong Chau Has A Net Worth Of $3 Million

Hong Chau, who portrayed the role of Liz on The Whale is worth $3 million. Chau doesn't need your approval; the Homecoming actress went from struggling with minor roles for years to bagging her breakthrough role in Downsizing.

Following her role in the 2017 movie, Chau earned lead roles in Driveways and American Woman. Outside the big screen, the Golden Globe Award nominee has had main roles in television series such as Homecoming, Watchmen, and The Night Agent.

2 Samantha Morton Has A Net Worth Of $10 Million

With a whopping net worth of $10 million Samantha Morton, who portrays Mary on The Whale comes a close second. There are several things you didn't know about Samantha Morton, perhaps the most intriguing one being that she earned her first Academy Award nomination for a role in which she didn’t speak.

The Walking Dead actress is a seasoned actress with many feathers to her hat. Morton has starred in several renowned projects, including Sweet and Lowdown, and In America.

1 Brendan Fraser Has A Net Worth Of $20 Million

With a staggering net worth of $20 Million, Brendan Fraser leads the pack as the cast member from The Whale with the most dough. The seasoned actor who lost his $45 million net worth to serial health and legal issues appears to be returning to promising days.

Fraser has multiple acting projects to his name, including The Mummy, and Gods and Monsters among others. Fraser's role in The Whale is the perfect comeback role for the actor since returning to the big screen in 2019.
