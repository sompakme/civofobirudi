Philip Michael Thomas Biography

Philip Michael Thomas is a retired American Actor and Musician who was born and brought-up in Columbus, Ohio. He is widely known for his roles as detective Ricardo Tubbs on the hit 1980s television series ‘Miami Vice’. 

After his success in Miami Vice, he featured in various made for television movies and advertisements for telephone psychic services. He worked as a spokesperson for cell phone entertainment company Next ones and supplied the voice for the role Lance Vance in the video games Grand Theft Auto: Vice City and Grand Theft Auto: Vice City Stories.
