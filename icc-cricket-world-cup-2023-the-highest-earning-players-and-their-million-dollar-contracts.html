ICC Cricket World Cup 2023: The Highest-Earning Players And Their Million-Dollar Contracts

 Posted on August 15, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1239 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Highlights

  • Shakib Al Hasan, with a net worth of $36 million, signed a $1.5 million contract to showcase his skills in the World Cup.
  • Temba Bavuma, with a net worth of $5 million, will lead South Africa with a $1 million contract, ready to conquer the stadium.
  • Virat Kohli, India's cricket icon, has a net worth of $112 million and signed a $5 million contract, proving his value to the sport and the event.

The ICC Cricket World Cup is one of the most celebrated events in the world, where fans from all corners of the world gather in one place to experience the apex of cricket talent.

First introduced in 1973, this event remains popular among millions of fans – who buy large screen televisions to watch, or travel to the match's location to watch their idols smashing sixes and breaking stumps. The Cricket stage does not just offer some thrilling matches between participating countries, but also reveals the stagging earnings of world class Cricket legends.

This article will examine the highest-earning megastars of the cricket world, who have made some lucrative contracts for the ongoing ICC Cricket World Cup 2023.

12 Shakib Al Hasan: Bangladesh's Cricketing Star

Bangladesh’s cricket whiz, Shakib Al Hasan, is ready to exhibit his unique spinning style and score boosting batting for this World Cup – with his squad of Bengal Tigers.

With a net worth of approximately $36 million, this cricket sensation signed a contract of $1.5 million to bring his remarkable skills to the World Cup.

11 Kane Williamson: New Zealand's Cool Captain

Famous for his megawatt smile and persistent batting, Kane Williamson will represent New Zealand as the Captain of an unyielding squad in the ICC World Cup 2023.

The notable captain and finalist of the previous World Cup has a net worth of approximately $10 million, and he’s representing his nation with a contract of $1.5 million in this globally watched event.

10 Babar Azam: Pakistan's Elegant Run-Machine

When cricket fans consider elegance on the pitch, Baber Azam inevitably comes to mind.

Distinguished for his classical strokes and exquisite style, the Captain of Pakistan’s team has secured his place in the top five of all formats of the ICC (International Cricket Council). Endorsed by giant global brands like Pepsi and HBL, Baber Azam possesses a net worth of $4 million – and he’s ready to show his excellence in this World Cup with a contract of $1 million.

9 Temba Bavuma: South Africa's Stormy Batsman

Notorious for his diminutive stature and explosive batting, Temba Bavuma is Captaining a fierce squad for South Africa, and they’re ready to conquer the stadium with skyscraping sixers.

A significant presence in the Cricket World, Temba Bavuma built an empire and net worth of $5 million with his tenacious hard work. With a contract of $1 million, Bavuma will emerge to the amphitheater of the modern world shaking with roaring cheers.

8 David Warner: Australia's Explosive Opener

David Warner, a popular Australian opener with his dynamite batting, takes the score to the highest peak when he steps onto the pitch.

The power hitter has earned millions of fans throughout his career and is in high spirits to represent the Australian team in this World Cup. According to reports, David Warner has an estimated net worth of approximately $13 million, and he reportedly signed a contract of $2 million for the ICC World Cup 2023.

7 Joe Root: England's Dependable Batsman

Joe Root – a significant player with the ICC World Cup 2022 Champion Team, England – is famous for his long-lasting strokes and dominance on the pitch.

Joe Root holds a net worth of an estimated $10 million, making him one of the richest cricketers in the world. Root, an "accuracy machine," stepped into this event with earnings exceeding $2 million.

6 Rashid Khan: Afghanistan's Spin Sensation

The master of spins and a top-ranked bowler in ICC, Rashid Khan, is representing his nation to show the world the top-class spin bowling.

An ordinary street boy who became a national hero, Khan now has a net worth of approximately $4 million. This spinning wizard signed a contract of $2 million to cast his spell on the ICC World Cup in 2023.

5 Pat Cummins: Australia's Premier Pacer

Pat Cummins, an Australian stumps-breaking phenomenon, is a recognized and key pacer of the dauntless squad.

Representing the Australian Team in the World Cup, Pat Cummins is the second most high-earning player at this colossal event, with a contract of $3 million. A master of extraordinary paces – who makes the management bring extra stumps, Pat Cummins – has a net worth of approximately $41 million.

4 Ben Stokes: England's All-Round Asset

England’s substantial all-rounder Ben Stokes can do magic both with his bat and ball, and he played a pivotal role in lifting the 2022 World Cup Trophy.

He’s one of the senior players who are still in their prime, diving all over the stadium and not letting even a single shot cross the boundaries. Because of his experience and remarkable skills, he signed a contract of $2 million for the much anticipated 2023 World Cup.

According to reports, Ben Stokes has a net worth of approximately $13 million.

3 Jasprit Bumrah: India's Bowling Tycoon

Feared by first-class batters, Jasprit Bumrah is India’s finest and ICC’s top-class fast bowler, who can best any batter with his explosive yorkers.

This idol of many fans was injured and stayed off the field for quite some time – but Bumrah rose again as a warrior, and made a comeback with a whopping $2 million contract. Besides dominating the pitch, Bumrah possesses a handsome net worth of $9 million.

2 Jonny Bairstow: England's Persistent Opener

Jonny Bairstow, a doppelganger for Ron Weasley of Harry Potter fame, is a persistent yet aggressive England opener who forgets to lose his wicket when you start enjoying his shots.

Bairstow is among cricket hotshots who can take this stage to its skyscraping limits, and that’s the reason he reportedly charged over $2 million for the 2023 World Cup. Jonny has an approximate net worth of $19 million.

1 Virat Kohli: India's Cricket Icon

Virat Kohli, India’s cricket icon, is the most followed South Asian celebrity with over 259 million Instagram followers.

This cricket virtuoso is widely lauded for his aggressive batting and robust fielding. The pitch celeb signed a contract of $5 million for the ICC World Cup 2023, evidence of his value to the sport and the event itself.

Besides reigning the cricket world, Virat Kohli boasts an enormous $112 million net worth.

The World of Cricket is full of intrigue, unforgettable highlights, field rivalries, and national pride, and the eminent cricketers mentioned above are taking this sport to extraordinary limits. Cricket clearly stands alone as a draw for millions of fans in countless countries, and the extravagant lifestyles of its athletes and their lucrative salaries can be equally fascinating.
