YouTube’s beauty sensations have been shaking up the place for a while now. They’ve been around since the inception of the platform. YouTube is by far the most popular video streaming platform and its influencers acquire massive followings. It’s the fastest, most effective way of getting all the info and guidance you need to transform your looks.

In this list, you’ll get to know what your favorite beauty coach has been up to aside from showing you different eyeliner techniques and trending fashion approaches. Go ahead and discover what’s going on in their romantic lives! Without further delay, here are YouTube’s 10 most popular beauty gurus and their love lives.

RCLBeauty101 - In A Relationship

Rachel Claire Levin is a Philadelphia born YouTuber, the most popular of the YouTube Beauty Influencers. She is currently in a relationship with Tyler Regan, an American Instagram star, who is rumored to have gained a massive audience on Instagram after he started dating Rachel (that’s how popular she is).

Rachel posted her first video in December 2010 and has since amassed a stunning 14.3 million subscribers on her YouTube channel, most of who are notably on the younger end of the spectrum. Rachel and Tyler started dating in 2017. She has also been in one previous relationship before she started dating Tyler. Rachel was also the highest-paid beauty YouTuber in 2018.

NikkieTutorials - Engaged

Nikkie de Jager is a popular Dutch makeup artist and vlogger. A YouTube beauty sensation with over 12.3 million subscribers. She is currently engaged to Dylan. Dylan proposed to Nikkie while on vacation in Italy and she accepted. There were lots of exciting online moments for the star who posted a lot of pictures, including one where she was wearing her glamorous engagement ring.

Nikkie is not only popular but also quite influential and has done videos featuring superstars, the likes of Kim Kardashian and Drew Barrymore and has also collaborated with lots of beauty brands including Maybelline & Ofra.

Zoella - In A Relationship

Though Zoe Elizabeth Sugg hasn’t been super-active on her main YouTube channel, there’s no denying the popularity and influence the beauty sensation has. With over 11.5 million subscribers on the platform, she is a force to be reckoned with. Zoe is in a romantic relationship with her longtime boyfriend, Alfie Deyes.

The couple recently celebrated their 7th anniversary and are reportedly engaged. Zoe is quite a busy bee and has also gotten into other endeavors – she’s published books and launched her beauty line in 2014 – which would explain why she hasn’t been as active as she was back in the day.

Huda Beauty - Married

Huda Kattan is an Oklahoma born YouTube beauty expert. Her YouTube channel, “Huda Beauty,” has over 3.67 million subscribers. Her Instagram account has even more followers (33+ million). She launched cosmetics brand Huda Beauty back in 2013. Huda was described as one of the ten most powerful influencers in the world of beauty by Forbes and was also listed as one of the 25 most influential people on the internet in 2017 by Time magazine.

Huda Kattan married her longtime boyfriend, Christopher Goncalo in 2009. The internet beauty star revealed a time she almost got divorced because she became so obsessed with her work. Though the relationship was saved, you can hardly blame her, considering the demands of her work.

Grav3yardgirl - In A Relationship

Rachel Marie Meyer, better known as Bunny, is one of the elites, albeit her dooming channel name, the beauty sensation has over 8.4 million YouTube subscribers and over 1.5 billion total views. She is known as the ‘Swamp Queen’ and ‘Bunny Meyer.’ The 34-year-old American vlogger is in a romantic relationship with longtime boyfriend, Micah James, popularly known as ‘Dogman.’

The two have been an item since 2004. Rachel is also a believer in the paranormal and often posts videos recounting experiences she has had (kinda goes with the name of her channel). Rachel has also posted videos that featured her boyfriend, Dogman. Her style is bubbly and quite active.

Carli Bybel - (Possibly) In A Relationship

Carli Sue Bybel is a 28-year-old American YouTube beauty expert. Her channel, Carli Bybel, has over 6.19 million subscribers. The star ended her relationship with fellow YouTuber Brett Cap and confirmed their separation in December 2017. In 2018 she posted a romantic photo of her and a man identified to be Badr Ghabi, on Instagram. Badr is said to be a real estate broker and model.

Though her fans have only been speculative of the relationship between the two and Badr has also posted a photo of the two of them together while on vacation in Morocco, there has been no direct confirmation by either of the two that they are dating or otherwise.

Dope2111 - Married

Promise Tamang Phan is a Nepalese-American beauty sensation, with over 5.94 million YouTube subscribers. She is no doubt in the league of the elite. The renowned beauty star is married to Steve Phan, having married in 2009. She posts a lot of photos of her and her husband on her Instagram account.

She also has a second YouTube channel, “Promis Phan,” where she has amassed over a million followers, a sure sign of her unstoppable spirit and ability to replicate her winnings. She is known for her elaborate makeup techniques where she transforms herself into popular celebrities and figures; it is always fun to watch her turn into celebrities like Beyoncé, Disney princesses, and others.

Meredith Foster - (Reportedly) Single

Meredith Elaine Foster is a 24-year-old Los-Angeles born YouTube beauty guru, with over 4.62 million subscribers. Meredith posts beauty videos and lifestyle videos on her self-titled channel. Meredith is reportedly not dating anyone at the moment and like many celebrities, she keeps her personal life private. The fans don’t like that a lot, but many stars do it if they can.

Meredith Foster also runs a secondary YouTube channel titled “vlogsbymeredith” (which has over 981 thousand subscribers) where she posts other kinds of content. The star is all about being confident in yourself and very often emphasizes ‘being comfortable in your own skin.’

Desi Perkins - Married

Desi Perkins is a popular American YouTube beauty sensation and self-proclaimed ‘YouTube Ninja.’ The Latina runs a self-named beauty channel that has amassed over 3.3 million subscribers. She is famous for her collaborations with Kim Kardashian and has also partnered with Lancôme, La Mer and MAC makeup lines.

Desi Perkins is married to Steven Perkins, a 3D artist and also a YouTube star. The two have been married to each other since 2012 and co-run a YouTube channel named “The Perkins.” The 32-year-old also launched her makeup line in collaboration with fellow beauty star Katy ‘Lustrelux’ Degroot and Dose of Colors.

Camila Coelho - Married

This Brazilian YouTube star, Camila Coelho, has been married to Icaro Coelho since 2010. She has two major beauty channels with over 3.4 million subscribers on her Portuguese-based channel and a stunning 1.2 million subscribers on her “Camilo Coelho English” channel, targeted for English speaking folks. Camila also featured her husband on one of her videos in 2013, where she asked him about some of his favorite things.

In the video, she describes how she and Icaro met through one of her friends when she was 17. Though Camila isn’t the biggest on this list, she is surely worthy of being one of the elites in the beauty world and has revealed she is still striving towards bigger accomplishments.
