12 Celebs Who Are Part Of Cameron Diaz's Circle (8 She Can't Stand)

 Posted on July 22, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  806 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Cameron Diaz has been in the business for quite some time, and with loads of years in Hollywood, comes loads of Hollywood friends. Despite the actress reportedly "retiring" from acting to focus on motherhood, we can't help but remember the amazing films Diaz starred in.

From 'Charlie's Angels' to 'The Holiday' and 'Bad Teacher', Cameron Diaz has been making us laugh, cry and feel an array of even more emotions for nearly 30 years. The actress first broke out on the scene in the 1994 film 'The Mask' and has been killing it ever since.

With an extensive resume and years in the entertainment industry, Cameron Diaz has become close to a number of her co-stars, however, she's also found herself in a feud or two over the course of her career. Here are 12 celebrities who are part of Cameron's inner circle, and 8 who the star can't stand!

40 Drew Barrymore - Best Of Friends

39

Drew Barrymore and Cameron Diaz have been friends for the longest time! The two first starred in the hit film 'Charlie's Angels' back in 2000 and have been close ever since. They not only remain in contact, but they see each other quite frequently, making their bond quite the special one.

38 Jennifer Lawrence & Adele - Friends That March Together, Stay Together

37

Cameron Diaz has a long repertoire of celeb friends, a few of which include none other than 'Hello' singer Adele and fellow actress Jennifer Lawrence. This trio was seen together back in 2017 at the Women's March just days after the presidential inauguration. You know what they say, friends that march together, stick together!

36 Leonardo DiCaprio - Hollywood Pals

35

Cameron Diaz and Hollywood's most eligible bachelor Leonardo DiCaprio are also close friends! The two starred in 'Gangs of New York' back in 2002 and were even rumoured to be dating. While neither of them has ever confirmed or denied said rumours, they've made it clear that they are very close friends.

34 Will Smith - Family Friends

33

Cameron and the entire Smith family have been keeping it tight for quite some time! Not only have the two been very close, but they also worked together on the 2014 'Annie' film, in which Cameron acted in and Will produced. They are also spotted sharing hugs and catching up at most high profile events and parties.

32 Nicole Richie - Sisters In Law

31

While this is not a pairing you'd expect, Cameron Diaz and Nicole Richie are more than just friends, they are sisters! Cameron Diaz is married to musician Benji Madden, whose twin brother Joel Madden is married to none other than Nicole Richie. The two may not be directly related, but they have definitely formed a loving relationship.

30 Julia Roberts - Friends Since The 90s

29

Julia Roberts and Cameron Diaz both broke out in the 90s and instantly hit it off! Not only was Roberts' role in 'Pretty Woman' her breakthrough role, but Diaz also found herself scoring major roles around the same time. The two would gab and catch up on the red carpet, over dinner or at events where they appeared to get along as if they'd been friends for years.

28 Lucy Liu - Friends Forever

27

Much like Drew Barrymore, Cameron Diaz and fellow 'Charlie's Angels' co-star Lucy Liu have also remained very close since the film debuted back in 1994. The three girls have been friends ever since, so much that they even attended and honoured Lucy Liu at her Hollywood Walk of Fame ceremony last year, says Today. 

26 Leslie Mann - They Keep It Tight

25

Leslie Mann and Cameron Diaz starred in the hilarious film 'The Other Woman', which came out in 2014. The two have been close prior to the film, but the press tour and time spent together promoting the film only solidified their friendship even more. The two had each other's backs in the film and definitely do off-screen too.

24 Justin Timberlake - Co-Stars To Friends

23

Cameron Diaz and Justin Timberlake have had quite the bumpy ride, but they are friends nevertheless. The two dated in the early 2000s for nearly 4 years, however, they split back in 2007, says People. Despite their split, they have remained close and even worked together in Cameron's film 'Bad Teacher', where they had a few romantic scenes with one another.

22 Gwyneth Paltrow - Friends For Years

21

Gwyneth Paltrow is just one of those actresses who just so happens to be friends with anyone and everyone in Hollywood, including our beloved Cameron Diaz. The two have been kicking old school for over two decades now and always have nothing but kind words to say about the other. This is a friendship we can definitely get behind.
