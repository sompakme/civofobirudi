Misfits Boxing 11 LIVE RESULT: Jarvis defeats BDave to become NEW champ, Dapper Laughs beats Simple

 Posted on September 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  338 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Room for improvement

It wasn't such a great night for TikTok sensation Simple Simon, who was knocked out by comedian Dapper Laughs within the first round after two quick knockdowns.

Dapper Laughs however, he will be looking for another opponent, he will want to move up the ranks here and onto a main card before long.

Fight of the night

Fight of the night has got to go to the finale, Jarvis vs BDave.

It was a spectacular fight, which saw Jarvis knock down his opponent within the first TEN seconds of the bout.

BDave however, did really well to come back from that and put on a good showing to see the fight out, and attack Jarvis himself.

It wasn't quite enough however, as Jarvis walked away with the belt before calling out Alex Wassabi and Nichlmao.

Jarvis speaks out after his win

"I wanted to give the best fight I could and I did that"

"The adrenaline, I couldn't feel it, he has probably got the hardest head I have ever hit."

"Nichlmao, Alex Wassabi, if you're watching let's get it!"

Jarvis beats BDave by unanimous decision!

All three judges scored the bout 54-50 in favour of Jarvis!

BDave did really well regardless, and can be proud of that performance despite the loss.

Who is next for Jarvis?

Jarvis vs BDave - Round 5

Into the final round now, as we get close to crowning a champion!

Both guys cardio is holding up nicely.

BDave has done incredibly well to recover from that first round knock down.

It goes the distance, surprisingly, and both fighters will be extremely proud of that performance.

Jarvis vs BDave - Round 4

A huge right hand from BDave in round four, and Jarvis is forced to fight here!

Jarvis has definitely managed the fight better than BDave, but the latter brings the entertainment factor without a doubt!

Jarvis hasn't stung BDave as much lately as the fight progresses.

He does get a nice combination done though as the end!
