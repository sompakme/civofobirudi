Coroner identifies man shot to death at motel near Carowinds; arrest made WSOC TV

 Posted on July 27, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  162 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

YORK COUNTY, S.C. — A 20-year-old man has been charged following a deadly shooting Wednesday afternoon in the lobby of the Sleep Inn off Carowinds Boulevard, near the theme park in Fort Mill.

[ALSO READ: Massive prostitution sting makes 15 arrests at hotel on state line]

There was a large police presence at the motel at Lakemont and Carowinds boulevards in York County.

One man was killed, the sheriff’s office confirmed. The coroner’s office identified him as 18-year-old Joshua Richardson-Ross, of Charlotte.

Deputies said Isaiah Jones walked into the lobby, shot Richardson-Ross, and then left. He was spotted a short time later walking toward a nearby QT.

Authorities were able to arrest him Wednesday night in Iredell County but no other information has been released.
