Real Housewives Of Atlanta: Net Worth Ranking For Every Wife From Season 1 To Now

 Posted on August 8, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1071 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

The Real Housewives of Atlanta debuted on the Bravo Network in 2008. It's the third installment in The Real Housewives franchise. The show portrays the ups and downs of the women featured on it. The housewives range from financially struggling yet keeping up appearances, to some who are up & coming, and those who are already highly successful and already established in what they do. One thing the women of The Real Housewives of Atlanta have is common is drama.

There's no denying that The Real Housewives of Atlanta lead seemingly glamorous lives. From their mansions to their exciting lives, the housewives appear to have it made. But what if some of it is just a facade? Maybe some of them aren't doing as well as portrayed on the show. We have dived into the depths of the internet to find out what these housewives net worth really is.

14 Lisa Wu Has A Net Worth Of $100,000

Actress, musician, and dancer– Lisa Wu is a multifaceted woman. She was one of the original cast of The Real Housewives of Atlanta alongside Nene Leakes, Kim Zolciak-Biermann, and Sheree Whitfield. Lisa was only on the Bravo hit show for two seasons and is the housewife with the lowest net worth, coming in at $100,000.

13 Porsha Williams Is Good For $400,000

Porsha Williams was once worth millions when married to former NFL player Kordell Stewart, but walked away with nothing following their split. The reality star is now good for $400,000. Portia has been a permanent fixture on The Real Housewives of Atlanta since joining the cast in the fifth season of the show.

12 Cynthia Bailey Is Valued At $500,000

Bravo signed Cynthia Bailey to The Real Housewives of Atlanta because they "wanted to bring more class to the cast, and feature more women that already had successful careers before the show." It was a smart move on the network's part. Bailey has had a long & successful modelling career and is valued at $500,000.

11 Claudia Jordan Has A $500,000 Fortune

Claudia Jordan's stint on The Real Housewives of Atlanta was brief. She starred on the seventh season and didn't return for the eighth, which was a shame. Jordan has since dipped her toes in movies and launched lipstick & handbag lines. All her ventures have built her a $500,000 nest egg.

10 Shereé Whitfield Has An Estimated Fortune Of $800,000

Sheree Whitfield has had financial problems which her co-stars always alluded to on The Real Housewives of Atlanta. Of course, Sheree never actually admitted to being in dire straits, despite rumors of having debt in taxes. Whitfield launched a clothing line called She By Sheree and has an estimated fortune of $800,000.

9 Kenya Moore Is Not Too Shabby With An $800,000 Net Worth

Former Miss USA turned reality TV star, Kenya Moore, is also an actress and entrepreneur. Not only is she running her own production company, Moore Media House, she made an exercise video & produced a hair care product line. Her Real Housewives of Atlanta income is a bonus because Kenya also has other income streams and has a net worth of $800,000.

8 DeShawn Snow Has A $1.5M Fortune

After one season on The Real Housewives of Atlanta, DeShawn Snow decided she had had enough of reality TV and did not come back for a second season. DeShawn ventured into the real estate business after that. In 2019, she was accused of fraud and a lawsuit was filed against her. With a $1.5 million fortune, DeShawn can certainly afford to settle her legal bills.

7 Kim Zolciak-Biermann Is Valued At $3M

After five seasons on The Real Housewives of Atlanta, Kim Zolciak-Biermann left the show. Bravo offered the reality star a spin off called Don't Be Tardy For The Party, paying homage to Kim's single of the same name. According to Radar, Kim was making $80,000 per episode. The star has a net worth of $3 million.

6 Phaedra Parks Has A Net Worth $3M

Fired from The Real Housewives of Atlanta for making false allegation against her former castmate, attorney Phaedra Parks learned a lesson and was lucky to have her law firm checks to fall back on. Losing her Real Housewives check may have stung, but this Georgia peach is doing just fine with her $3 million fortune.

5 Eva Marcille Is Good For $4M

Eva Marcille got her big break on America's Next Top Model and won cycle three. Eva is an accomplished model who has graced magazine covers and walked prestigious runways. The multi-faceted star has dipped her toes in acting as well as appearing on The Real Housewives of Atlanta. Her net worth is estimated at $4 million.

4 Shamari DeVoe Has A $4M Net Worth

Shamari DeVoe is no stranger to fame, much like Kandi Burruss. Shamari was part of a popular R&B group called Blaque back in the day. Despite only starring on one season of The Real Housewives of Atlanta, she made serious bank. According to The Bounce, Shamari made $250,000 on real housewives and has a net worth of $4 million.

3 Kim Fields Is Valued At $8M

Kim Fields only appeared on one season of The Real Housewives of Atlanta. It doesn't really matter because she is a star in her own right and is doing just fine without the housewives' check. Kim is a famous actress and has starred on a number of sitcoms, most notably The Facts of Life and Living Single, and has a net worth of $8 million.

2 NeNe Leakes Has An Estimated Fortune Of $14M

The Real Housewives of Atlanta was merely a launchpad for Nene Leakes. The exposure she got in reality television opened her up to lucrative opportunities. Nene Leakes has done it all, from featuring on Glee and The New Normal, to appearing on multiple reality shows. Heck, she even launched a shopping line with The Home Network.

1 Kandi Burruss Has A Net Worth $30M

Kandi Burruss was famous way before Bravo knocked on her door, as she was part of the highly successful R&B girl group Xscape and a songwriter & record producer. To top it off, Kandi is also a savvy business woman, and she owns an adult toy company & a restaurant. The star has a net worth of $30 million.
