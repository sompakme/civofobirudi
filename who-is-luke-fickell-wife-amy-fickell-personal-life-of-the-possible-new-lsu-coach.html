Who Is Luke Fickell Wife Amy Fickell?

Amy Fickell is the wife of American football coach Luke Fickell. Professionally she is a physiotherapist who earned her physical therapy degree from the university in Ohio State. 

Since they were a sophomore at Ohio State, the couple has been dating, and they finally got married in 2000. The pair has six children together. 

Amy is a robust support system in her family, and Luke values her support and concern for the family.

In every other interview, Luke does not miss a chance to appreciate her for being a wonderful wife and a mother. 

Luke further adds in one of his interviews that she has a lot on her plate, being the mother of seven children, including him as a one. He says, “She’s got six genuine ones and me as the seventh big baby, so she’s got a lot on her hands.”

She does not seem to mind having a lot of work pressure and accepts the way things are. “I just get it done here as he has a lot,” Amy explained. “He has a lot on his plate, so that’s just how we work.”

Luke is fortunate to have such a loving and understanding wife.

Luke Fickell Recent Contract Renewal Has Increased His Net Worth 

Cincinnati and Luke Fickell have agreed to expand the contract to the 2026 season, raising the head coach’s annual salary to $3.4 million.

Fickell’s new contract has made him one of the top 40 highest-paid college football coaches.

Fickell will also receive an additional salary of $250,000 and supplemental compensation payments for his participation in television, radio, personal appearances, and private and public speaking engagements under the terms of the agreement.
