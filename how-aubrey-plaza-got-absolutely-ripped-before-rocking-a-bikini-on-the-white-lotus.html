How Do Most Celebrities Get So Fit?

Thanks to the fact that movie stars are often asked to play action stars or superheroes, it is common for famous actors to be in mind-blowing shape. As a result, it can be easy for observers to forget how much stars like Chris Hemsworth put in to be so ripped.

In order for stars like Hugh Jackman or Angelina Jolie to have such incredible bodies, there are two things they do. First off, the stars strictly control what they eat and drink, and they then adopt difficult workout routines that are specially formulated to produce the gains they desire.

While eating well and working out is obvious advice for anyone who wants to transform their body, every once in a while stars do strange things to get in shape.

Then there is Kelly Osbourne who hula-hoops and Gwyneth Paltrow who somehow does yoga without gravity.

How Did Aubrey Plaza Get Into Incredible Shape?

Back when Parks and Recreation fans were first introduced to Aubrey Plaza, most of them must have assumed there was no way she is as sarcastic as her character April was. As it turns out, however, it could easily be argued that Plaza is far more sarcastic than her April ever was.

Related: Aubrey Plaza Has A Theory Over Her Awkwardness With Stems From Her Past

As a result of how sarcastic Plaza often is, a lot of people don't know how to take her. In fact, it once was reported that her agent had to tell Plaza to tone it down around Robert De Niro since the acting legend was struggling to handle her antics.

While many fans feel that Plaza has a delightful sense of humor, it can be hard to get her to answer questions seriously at times. For that reason, it likely won't surprise anyone that when it comes to Plaza's fitness routine, it is hard to know exactly what she does.

Unlike stars like Dwayne Johnson who love to reveal the details of their workout regime, Plaza is cagey at best. However, even though it is impossible to detail how Plaza has sculpted her body, that doesn't mean that nothing is known about how she got so fit.

The first thing to know about Plaza's workout regimen is that she has remarked in the past that she struggles to do the same exercises on any kind of ongoing basis. As a result, it seems safe to assume that she mixes things up.

More importantly, when Plaza was interviewed by The Cut, she explained what being healthy means to her. "Wellness, to me, is: Being conscious of what you’re putting into your body and on your body, and taking care of yourself."

As a result of that belief, Plaza has revealed that one of the ways she stays healthy is by consuming a great deal of tea and water. During that same interview with The Cut, Plaza detailed how she likes to work out when she isn't in the gym.

According to Plaza, she was a big fan of staying fit by playing basketball and practicing yoga. "I love yoga — that’s probably my favorite exercise because I’m not so much into cardio."

Another one of the ways that Plaza stays healthy is by taking her dogs for walks. In fact, Plaza stays active walking her dogs so often that she has joked about how many photos the paparazzi have of her dogs, including pictures of her picking up after the animals.
