Joel Schiffman, born and raised in the U.S in 1958 in a Christian family, is a famous entrepreneur and director of Janus Financial Group. 

Early Life Of The Famous Entrepreneur Joel Schiffman 

He is also known to be the senior relationship manager of Columbia Management’s national account that is located in the city of New York. 

He pursued a bachelor’s in economics from the University of California and graduated in 1981. He also attained a certificate in Investment Analysis from the University of Pennsylvania. 

The famous financier and American was promoted to Head of Intermediary Distribution, North America at Schroder Investment Management. 

The Split of Joel Schiffman and Hoda Kotb

The financier was engaged to Hoda Kotb in November 2019 after dating for six years. 

After the questions came into Hoda for not wearing her engagement ring,  Hoda revealed that she and Joel had called off their engagement. 

“Joel and I have had a lot of prayerful and really meaningful conversations over the holidays, and we decided that we’re better as friends and parents than we are as an engaged couple,” Hoda said to Today’s co-host Jenna Bush Hager.

Hoda continued: “We decided that we are going to start this new year and begin it kind of on our new path as loving parents to our adorable, delightful children, and as friends. It’s not like something happened. They say sometimes relationships are meant to be there for a reason or a season or for a lifetime. And I feel like ours was meant to be there for a season.”

She added, “He’s a great guy. He’s a very kind and loving person and I feel privileged to have spent eight years with him. We’re both good and we’re both kind of going on our way on our path, and we’ll be good parents to those two lovely kids.”

In a recent interview, she also revealed that the couple is better off as friends and co-parents to their two adopted daughters, aged 4 and 2 respectively. 

The couple started dating in the summer of 2013 when Hoda was speaking at an event for Wall Street Professionals and book launch after the show.

The couple decided to keep their relationship private until 2015 when ‘The Daily Mail’ published their photos together. 

The couple moved in together in 2016; Hoda talked about it on her SiriusXM series, The Hoda Show. 

The struggle of Starting a Family

After going through breast cancer, Hoda could not conceive; therefore, the couple decided to start a family by adopting their daughters.

The couple welcomed their first child through adoption in February 2017, a daughter named Haley Joy. 

The second daughter Hope Catherine of the couple, came via adoption too in April 2019.

The Proposal

The couple announced their engagement in 2019 when they were vacationing in Mexico. Joel proposed to her during an intimate dinner around the beach. 

“We ended up having a little dinner on the beach,” she said on Today, clarifying that the two were in the middle of a tropical vacation. “And Joel was like, ‘I have something else I would like to say,’ and he said some beautiful things, and then he got down on one knee. Then he said, ‘Would you be my wife?’

The couple did not tie the knot as both of them were first married to someone else, and Joel even has a daughter named Kyle from his first marriage. Hoda was also married to a tennis coach named Burzis Kanga. Therefore, they were just living together and not in a hurry for marriage. 

The couple might not be seen together, but they will be working alongside their daughters.

Joel Schiffman’s Career

Joel Schiffman started his career by serving in Investment firms.

The American financier and national worked at Kidder Peabody as a senior vice president from 1987 to 1994. 

From 1994 to 2003, he was appointed as a consulting group director at Smith Barney. 

In November 2003, he joined Trail Ridge Capital, LLC as a Principal, Director of Marketing and worked until September 2005. 

He also worked as senior vice president, Business development, and client services at Reed, Conner, and Birdwell from September 2005 to May 2008. 

From 2008 to 2015, he served as Director, recruitment Platform Relationship and senior relationship manager at various firms.

After gaining experience from several places, he worked as a Vice President and Director at Janus Henderson Investor U.S for four years. From 2015 to 2019. 

Later in 2019, he joined as a head of US defined Contribution and Insurance Sales at Schroder Investment Management and worked till 2020.

He was then promoted to Head intermediary Distribution, North America.
