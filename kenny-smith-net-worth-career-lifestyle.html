Kenny Smith | NBA 

Former sports star Kenny Smith is one of the earliest NBA players to gain success and fame in the basketball championships.

Smith started his professional career with the Sacramento Kings in 1987. The team picked him sixth overall in the NBA draft.

Likewise, he landed the NBA All-Rookie Team spot.

Then he stayed with the Kings from 1989 to 1990 before arriving at the Huston Rockets.

Playing with the Rockets made him win two NBA championships.

What’s more, he was recognized as McDonald’s All-American for his basketball skills.

The legendary sportsman officially took retirement from basketball in 1997, finishing the remainder with the Nuggets.
