She was the one that Jimmy would always try to make laugh first and foremost and would talk to her at least 3 times a day despite his busy schedule. A touching tribute was given to Carson Daly’s own late-night show as his mother also died in 2017.

Gloria Fallon was married to James Fallon Sr. She has a son Jimmy Fallon, who is a comedian, actor, and late-night television host and a daughter also named Gloria Fallon who is an author.
