Why Was A Major Star Written Out Of 'Hocus Pocus 2'?

 Posted on July 23, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  923 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Halloween is only a few months away, which means it's time to nearly start planning your next costume and decide who you're going to watch the 'Hocus Pocus' sequel with.

The 1993 movie from director Kenny Ortega is getting a first sequel once again focused on the villainous Sanderson sisters wreaking havoc on modern-day Salem, Massachusetts. However, while some cast members of the first cult classic film are returning for this second chapter nearly three decades later, there's one beloved character that almost reprise their role... until they didnt.

Thora Birch, who played protagonist Max Dennison's (Omri Katz) sassy little sister Dani, almost returned for the sequel, set to debut later this year. However, while original plans for the new movie included a good role for Dani in adult-form, Birch had to decline due to scheduling conflicts.

Why Isn't Thora Birch's Dani In 'Hocus Pocus 2'?

Birch has recently explained why she won't be returning as Dani Dennison in the 'Hocus Pocus' sequel directed by Anne Fletcher ('The Proposal,' '27 Dresses') and written by Jen D'Angelo.

"Everybody tried so hard. Everybody tried so hard to make that happen and it just didn't, but everybody tried so hard," Birch told 'ComicBook.com' about her possible return.

"That was everybody's intention," she added.

While production and fans would love to see Birch reprise the role of Dani, the actress revealed she would be more interested in playing another character in the franchise.

"But you know what? At the end of the day, just between you and I, I'd probably rather play Winifred in the future. I'd probably rather play Winnie," Birch said of the astute Sanderson witches leader played by Bette Midler.

Thora Birch Has Exited Netflix Series 'Wednesday'

At the end of last year, it was revealed that Birch exited Netflix's upcoming series 'Wednesday.' Directed by Tim Burton, the series will zero in on the adolescence of Wednesday Addams, played by 'You' star Jenna Ortega.

"Thora has returned to the States to attend to a personal matter and will not be returning to the production," a representative for series producer MGM said in a statement to 'Deadline' in December 2021.

Prior to her departure, Birch had already filmed most of her scenes as Tamara Novak, Wednesday's dorm mother at Nevermore Academy. As per IMDb, Birch is scheduled to appear in six out of eight episodes.

While Birch will still appear on 'Wednesday,' a surprise return as Dani in 'Hocus Pocus 2' doesn't seem to be on the table.

Who Will Return for 'Hocus Pocus 2'?

Dani may not be returning to Salem in the new instalment of 'Hocus Pocus,' but there are other major stars that will reprise their roles, much to the delight of fans.

Of course, Midler, Kathy Najimi and Sarah Jessica Parker will make their long-anticipated return as the Sanderson trio: Winifred aka Winnie, Mary and Sarah.

Doug Jones will also make an appearance in the role of Billy Butcherson, introduced in the first movie. The character is an old boyfriend Winnie poisoned in 1693 and resurrected as a zombie three centuries later.

As for Katz and Shaw, it doesn't look like they will return, but fans better keep their eyes peeled for a very unexpected cameo.

What Birch Has Said About Starring On 'Hocus Pocus'

Birch, who starred on 'Hocus Pocus' when she was around ten, has fond memories of playing Dani.

"Me and Vinessa, whenever we see each other, it's like old friends getting back together. She's just a wonderful person."
