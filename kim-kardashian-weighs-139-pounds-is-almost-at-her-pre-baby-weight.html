Kim Kardashian Weighs 139 Pounds, Is Almost at Her 'Pre-Baby Weight'

 Posted on August 1, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  168 words  |  Martina Birk

One final push! Kim Kardashian has been working hard to slim down following the December birth of her baby boy, Saint — and it’s paying off. The ubiquitous reality star revealed on Wednesday, April 27, that she’s just a couple of pounds shy of her pre-baby weight.

“YOU GUYS!!!! PRE BABY WEIGHT WAS 135!!!!!” Kardashian, 35, captioned a photo via Snapchat. In the pic, Kardashian stands on a scale, which shows that she currently weighs 139.6 pounds.

Kardashian has made her health a priority since she and husband Kanye West welcomed Saint four months ago. Earlier this week, she posted a video of herself and the rapper, 38, running sprints and shared photos of her lean meals.

“Temptation but I’m focused,” she captioned a photo of chicken and waffles on Sunday. As an alternative, Kardashian ate eggs and fruit.
