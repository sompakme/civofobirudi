Bob Eubanks Ex-Wife: Who is Irma Eubanks?

 Posted on August 12, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  193 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Irma Eubanks is the ex-wife of Bob Eubanks an American television personality and game show host.

The former couple married in a ceremony in 1969. Trace, Corey, and Theresa were their three children. Although it is not clear if Bob and Irma ever divorced, they split sometime in the early ‘90s, and he started dating his future second wife, Deborah James. Mrs Eubanks died in 2002 at the age of 77. Her obituary stated that she had long battled a deadly disease. She was laid to rest in Santa Barbara County, California.

Bob Eubanks is best known for his role as the host of the popular game show “The Newlywed Game,” which aired from 1966 to 1974 and was later revived in various formats. The show featured recently married couples answering questions about their spouses to win prizes.

Eubanks began his career in radio in the 1950s before transitioning to television hosting. Apart from “The Newlywed Game,” he also hosted other game shows such as “Rhyme and Reason” and “Card Sharks.” Eubanks was known for his friendly and affable hosting style, making him a familiar face on television during the 1960s and 1970s.
