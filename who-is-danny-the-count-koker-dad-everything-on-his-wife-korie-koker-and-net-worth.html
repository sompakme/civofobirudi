Danny “The Count” Koker, popular for running the automobile shop Count’s Kustoms, is the son of a celebrated musician Daniel Koker Senior. 

Danny Koker, a native of Cleveland, Ohio, is an accomplished car restorer and reality television star.

He is prominently known as the owner of Count’s Kustoms. It is an automobile company that works on the restoration and customization of cars.

Mr. Koker has appeared as a recurring expert on Pawn Stars, a Las Vegas-based reality series that chronicled the daily activity of a World Famous Gold & Silver Pawn Shop.

As per his IMDb, he is also featured in the Pawn Stars’ spin-off series, Counting Cars, where it revolves around the daily activity of Count’s Kustoms.

Who Is Danny Koker aka The Count Dad?

Danny Koker’s dad is the legendary musician and singer, Daniel Nicholas Koker Senior.

His father, Daniel was a baritone vocalist and pianist. He was a part of numerous bands like Foggy River Boys, The Cathedral Quartet, Weatherford Quartet, The Koker Family Singers, and more.

Besides music, he owned a television station in Las Vegas. He also served in the U.S. Army as a Green Beret with the 10th Special Forces Group and played hockey when he was in the army.

Danny, whose real name is Daniel Nicholas Koker II, was named after his father.

Meet Danny Koker Wife Korie Koker

Danny Koker is married to his wife Korie Koker.

Korie’s maiden name is Korie Fera and she was born on August 8, 1965. She grew up in Chicago and has a degree in Broadcasting.

She worked as a camera operator and production coordinator for a local television station after she finished her graduation.

Korie is also a singer like her husband. She was a part of a musical band but it disbanded shortly after it was formed.

She and Danny met in Nevada, Las Vegas, and eventually started dating. They married in a private wedding ceremony in 2015.

What Is Danny Koker Net Worth 2021?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Danny Koker has an estimated net worth of $13 million.

He generated most of his huge fortune from his work as a car restorer and his automobile restoration company Count’s Kustoms.

In addition, he has a successful career in television. He co-owned a local television station where he hosted a show called, Saturday Fright at the Movies.

Celebrity Net Worth reported his salary was around $100,000 per episode during his time on Counting Cars, meaning he earns around $1.6 million per year from the reality series alone.

Koker also has several other businesses including Count’s Vamp’d Rock Bar and Grill and Count’s Tattoo Company.

Danny Koker Age & Wikipedia Explored

Danny Koker, age 57, was born on January 5, 1964. He was born in Cleveland but he also spent time in Detroit while growing up.

Many of his family members worked at the Ford company and he was exposed to cars from an early age. This led him to known everything about the automobile industry.

He further gained knowledge about mechanical skills by himself which became a huge asset for running his business currently.

Aside from being a businessman, he is an equally accomplished musician. He took after his father Danny Koker Sr. and has performed at Carnegie Hall.
