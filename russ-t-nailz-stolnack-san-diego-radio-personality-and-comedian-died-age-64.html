Russ T Nailz Stolnack San Diego Radio Personality and Comedian Died Age 64

 Posted on July 6, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  458 words  |  Larita Shotwell

What happened to the Russ T Nailz Stolnack? Here’s what we know about the San Diegan comedian.

Russ T Nailz Stolnack was an American comedian.

He was well known for his humorous impressions of Anchorman movies’ Ron Burgundy character and other musical stylings.

Russ T Nailz Stolnack Death – What Happened To Him?

Russ T Nailz Stolnack died on 9 March 2022.

At the time of his death, he was 64. The confirmation came from various sources, including his sister, Barbara Stolnack Luke, and radio brother Chris Cantore. She posted about her little brother’s passing about 10 hours ago on Facebook.

Likewise, Nailz’s former workplace, 91X radio, also shared the news on Twitter, expressing their sadness on the loss of the legendary San Diegan. Similarly, on Twitter, Former San Diego Mayor Kevin Faulconer called him a true San Diego legend and a man who had a heart of gold.

Despite all the confirmations from different sources, the cause of Nailz’s death remains unknown. None of them have mentioned anything.

Russ T Nailz Stolnack Wikipedia And Net Worth

Wikipedia is yet to feature comedian and radio personality Russ T Nailz Stolnack.

He has been doing comedy since 1957 and won The National Speakers Associations Humorous Speaker Competition, a Cable Ace Award, and six regional Emmy Awards.

According to his official Facebook account, Nailz began his career in 1957 at Comedian.RasisFunny. Similarly, he is a former on-air personality at KPRI Radio 106.7 FM (March 1978 to 1980), where he hosted Open Mic at Comedy Store La Jolla and Rock and Religion.

Besides that, he used to work at 91X – San Diego doing the first-morning show on The Rock of the 80s, Nailz and The JET. And before his death, he used to work at The Big Biz Show as a host/producer and Live High LIVE! as an executive producer, co-creator, and host.

According to his bio on RussisFunny, he was writing a book whose first chapter was titled Get Married and Have Kids. Similarly, he was an entertaining musician, beginning on 26 November 2021. A rendition of the 1995 Bobby Darin’s hit song Dream Lover is the last video on his YouTube.

Russ was also one of AuctionHelp’s partners, delivering an entertaining LIVE experience.

As for his net worth, the exact figure remains unknown. However, Salary.com says that the average comedian earns about $47 thousand in San Diego.

Russ T Nailz Stolnack Wife

The identity of Russ T Nailz Stolnack’s wife remains a mystery.

The comments under the latest post of Nina Detrow on Facebook about Nailz suggest that the two were in a relationship. However, we don’t know whether they were married or not.

Nonetheless, his social media suggest that he had two daughters, Bre and Alli Falletta, and one son, Tyrone Bone.
